Describe the Song
"Who Knows", by Daniel Caesar, is a calm and emotional song. It talks about love, feelings, and uncertainty in a relationship. In the song, Daniel Caesar feels afraid because he does not know if he is good enough for the other person. Even with these doubts, he still has hope that the relationship can work.

The song also talks about being honest with emotions and wanting love and support from someone special. The phrase "who knows?" means that nobody knows the future, but sometimes it is important to try and take risks for love.
About song
I discovered "Who Knows", because of a friend.
She told me to listen to this song, so I listened to it and I liked it very much.

At first, I did not understand the lyrics because the song was in English, but I loved the melody.
Later, my friend was showing me the lyrics of the song, and I identified with them.
What It Reminds Me Of
The song Who Knows reminds me of loving someone a lot and being afraid of losing them. When someone is very important to me, I feel scared of saying something wrong or not being good enough. The song makes me think about love and fear.
When I Like Listening to It
I like to listen to this song at night, in my room, when I am studying, on the bus looking at the window on a rainy day, at college when the class is boring, while I am washing the dishes, and while I am taking a shower.
